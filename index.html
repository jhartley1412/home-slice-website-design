“What’s up Home Slice?”

Authentic NY Style pizza by the slice or the pie

Home Slice Pizza is an independent neighborhood pizza joint serving authentic NY-style pizza—by the pie or by the slice—to nice people like you. We offer our homemade, hand tossed, bona fide pies for either dine in or carry out. Want us to donate something to your event? Giving back is totally our thing so please email us and we'll be in touch. Want to share your experiences, feedback, suggestions, hopes, fears, dreams, with us? We'd love to hear from you.
